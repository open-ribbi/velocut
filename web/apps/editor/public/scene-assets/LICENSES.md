Every asset vendored into this directory is license-vetted at vendoring time
and recorded here. Only licenses that permit unrestricted redistribution are
accepted: CC0 / public domain, or attribution-style licenses (CC-BY) with the
attribution recorded below. Nothing with redistribution restrictions (e.g.
Mixamo) ever enters this directory.
